SwiftMotion

COEH

Northern California Center for Occupational and Environmental Health (COEH)


I was hired as the organization's sole website developer to fix issues on their website. Due to client's preferences, I built this website using Wix as it was easier to maintain once I left.


Utilizing Adobe Illustrator, I redid the logo and most of the graphics for the website. Using web archives and backups, I reconstructed the lost webpages. I worked with the organization's program directors on adjusting the content to better reflect the organization's mission. Content migration was the hardest part but I succeeded.

Engineering Student Council


While a university student, I was the web master for the Engineering Student Council. I remade the website due to branding issues and backend infrastructure problems.


Built off of Sass, CSS, HTML5, and Vanilla Js, the new website is a resource hub for incoming and current students. The content management system is handled by the Open Computing Facility. The biggest challenge was ensuring that the next web master knew how to properly update the website.

Who? Guess!

Who? Guess!


Bored by the lack of games available online at the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic, a couple friends and I built a low fidelity version of the Guess Who game.


I used HTML5, CSS, and Vanilla Js to make it easier for someone with little coding experience to be able to modify the game. The biggest challenge was making the game playable no matter the screen-size.

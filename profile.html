Profile


Hi! I am a Masters of Science student in the Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering on the Product Development Engineering track at USC. While there, I was a researcher at the Center for Advanced Manufacturing focusing on multi-robot assemblies. I got my Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering and Operations Research from UC Berkeley where I also worked as a research student at the UC Berkeley/UC San Francisco Ergonomics Program.

Founded to represent Engineers at Berkeley


Our mission is to represent the engineering student body; support engineering societies in reaching their full potential;
and promote a diverse community through successful collaboration between students, faculty, societies, and the College of Engineering.

Next

Founded in 1974 as the Engineer's Joint Council

Our mission still stands true to the days EJC existed

Since the founding of the Engineer's Joint Council, we have continually supported the engineering student body at Berkeley through advocating for student to the Collge of Engineering, supporting over 85 engineering student organization, and hosting events to bring together the engineering community.

The ESC is responsible for handling the funding for the ASUC in terms of dispersing funds to engineering student organizations. We also host one of the largest organization spaces on campus as well as host Engineer's Week.
